About BCNSHS

Bais City National Science High School is a government-supported secondary school located in Bais City, Negros Oriental. It is designed to provide quality education with a strong emphasis on science, mathematics, and research, preparing students for future studies and careers in science-related fields.

No To Drugs

Bais City National Science High School strongly supports the campaign against illegal drugs. We aim to guide the youth toward a safe, healthy, and drug-free lifestyle.
